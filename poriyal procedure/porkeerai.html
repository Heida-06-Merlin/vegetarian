Keerai Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped spinach or any leafy greens (keerai)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds, urad dal, and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, and minced garlic. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped spinach or leafy greens. Cook until they wilt and become tender.
  • Add salt to taste and mix well.
  • Finally, add grated coconut and stir for a minute.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish with rice or roti.
