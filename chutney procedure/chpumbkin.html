Pumpkin Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup tamarind pulp
  • 2 tablespoons jaggery or brown sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add cumin seeds and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  • Add diced pumpkin and sauté for a few minutes until it starts to soften.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook until the pumpkin is completely cooked and soft.
  • Add tamarind pulp and jaggery (or brown sugar). Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  • Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend until smooth.
  • Adjust seasoning if necessary.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve with dosa, idli, rice, or any other dish of your choice.
