Paal Pongal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice (preferably short-grain rice like ponni rice)
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup jaggery (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • A handful of cashews and raisins (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Procedure

  • Wash rice and moong dal together and drain.
  • In a pressure cooker, add the washed rice and moong dal along with water and milk. Cook for 4-5 whistles until well cooked and mushy.
  • Once cooked, mash the rice and dal mixture slightly with a ladle or spoon.
  • In a separate pan, melt jaggery with a little water to make a syrup. Strain to remove impurities.
  • Heat ghee in a small pan and fry cashews and raisins until golden. Keep aside.
  • Add the jaggery syrup to the cooked rice and dal mixture. Mix well.
  • Add cardamom powder and fried cashews and raisins. Mix again.
  • Simmer for a few minutes until everything blends well together.
  • Paal Pongal is ready to be served hot. Enjoy!
