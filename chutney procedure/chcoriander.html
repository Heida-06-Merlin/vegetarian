Coriander Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh coriander leaves, washed and chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, washed and chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated coconut (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Water, as needed

Procedure

  • In a blender or food processor, combine coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chilies, grated coconut (if using), cumin seeds, salt, sugar (if using), and lemon juice.
  • Blend until smooth, adding water as needed to reach the desired consistency.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Coriander chutney is ready to serve. Enjoy with your favorite snacks or dishes!
