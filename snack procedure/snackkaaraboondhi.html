Kaara Boondhi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, rice flour, baking soda, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
  • Add water gradually to make a smooth, lump-free batter with a pouring consistency.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
  • Hold a perforated ladle or boondhi maker over the hot oil.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter over the ladle and spread it evenly. Tap gently so that small droplets fall into the hot oil.
  • Fry until the boondhis turn golden brown and crispy. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • Once all the boondhis are fried and drained, allow them to cool completely.
  • Store in an airtight container and serve as a snack or use in various chaat recipes.
