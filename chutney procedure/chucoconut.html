Coconut Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup roasted chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup water, or as needed

Procedure

  • In a blender or food processor, combine grated coconut, chopped green chilies, roasted chana dal, fresh cilantro leaves, tamarind paste, and salt.
  • Add water gradually and blend until smooth. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Optionally, you can prepare tempering by heating some oil in a small pan, adding mustard seeds, urad dal (split black gram), curry leaves, and dried red chilies. Pour this tempering over the chutney for added flavor.
  • Serve coconut chutney with dosa, idli, vada, or any other South Indian snack.
