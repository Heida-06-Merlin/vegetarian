Kaara Paniyaram

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dosa batter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Curry leaves, a few, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for greasing the paniyaram pan

Procedure

  • Heat a paniyaram pan and grease each mold with oil.
  • Temper mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves in a small pan.
  • In a mixing bowl, add dosa batter, chopped onions, green chilies, grated coconut, chopped coriander leaves, asafoetida, tempered spices, and salt. Mix well.
  • Pour spoonfuls of the batter into each mold of the paniyaram pan.
  • Cook on medium heat until the bottom side turns golden brown.
  • Flip the paniyarams using a skewer or spoon and cook until the other side is also golden brown and crisp.
  • Remove from the pan and serve hot with chutney or sambar.
