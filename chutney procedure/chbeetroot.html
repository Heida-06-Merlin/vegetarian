Beetroot Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized beetroot, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 dry red chili
  • Few curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal, chana dal, dry red chili, and curry leaves. Sauté until dals turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped green chilies and grated beetroot. Sauté for 3-4 minutes until beetroot softens slightly.
  • Add grated coconut and tamarind paste. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Turn off the heat and allow the mixture to cool down.
  • Transfer the cooled mixture to a blender or food processor. Add salt to taste.
  • Blend until smooth, adding a little water if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Your delicious Beetroot Chutney is ready to serve! Enjoy it as a side dish with dosa, idli, or rice.
