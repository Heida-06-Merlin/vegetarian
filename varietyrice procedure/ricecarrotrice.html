Carrot Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add grated carrots and cook until they become soft.
  • Season with turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice to the pan. Mix everything gently until well combined.
  • Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat to allow the flavors to blend.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Carrot rice is ready to be served. Enjoy!
