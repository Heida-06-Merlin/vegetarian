Peerkangai Thogayal

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized ridge gourd (peerkangai), peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 4-5 dried red chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan. Add urad dal, dried red chilies, mustard seeds, and cumin seeds. Sauté until dal turns golden brown.
  • Add chopped ridge gourd and sauté until it becomes soft and cooked.
  • Turn off the heat and let it cool down slightly.
  • In a grinder, add the cooked ridge gourd mixture, grated coconut, tamarind paste, and salt. Grind into a coarse paste without adding water.
  • Heat the remaining oil in the same pan. Add a pinch of asafoetida.
  • Add the ground mixture to the pan and sauté for a couple of minutes until it thickens slightly.
  • Remove from heat and let it cool.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  • Your delicious Peerkangai Thogayal is ready to be served with rice or as a side dish for dosa or idli.
