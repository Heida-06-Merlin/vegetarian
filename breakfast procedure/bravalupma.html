Aval Upma

Ingredients

  • 2 cups thick or thin poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Lemon wedges for serving (optional)

Procedure

  • Rinse poha under cold water using a strainer. Drain well and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, green chili, ginger, peanuts, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add turmeric powder and sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add rinsed poha and salt to the pan. Mix well to combine.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Remove the lid, add grated coconut (if using), and mix well.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves (optional).
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges on the side.
