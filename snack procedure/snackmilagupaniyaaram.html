Milagu Paniyaram

Ingredients

  • 1 cup idli/dosa batter
  • 1 tablespoon black peppercorns, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil for greasing the paniyaram pan

Procedure

  • Heat a paniyaram pan and grease each mold with a little oil.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine idli/dosa batter, crushed black peppercorns, cumin seeds, chopped curry leaves, coriander leaves, grated ginger, chopped green chili (if using), and salt. Mix well.
  • Pour the batter into each mold, filling them about 3/4 full.
  • Cover the pan and cook the paniyarams on low to medium heat for about 2-3 minutes.
  • Once the bottom side turns golden brown, flip the paniyarams using a spoon or skewer.
  • Cook the other side until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the paniyarams from the pan and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • Serve hot with coconut chutney or any chutney of your choice.
