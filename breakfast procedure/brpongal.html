Pongal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow moong dal (lentils)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup cashews, chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash rice and moong dal together and set aside.
  • In a pressure cooker, heat ghee over medium heat.
  • Add cumin seeds, chopped ginger, green chilies, curry leaves, and chopped cashews. Saut√© until cashews turn golden brown.
  • Add washed rice and dal mixture to the pressure cooker. Mix well.
  • Add water, salt, and asafoetida. Stir to combine.
  • Close the pressure cooker lid and cook on medium-high heat until 3-4 whistles.
  • Once pressure releases naturally, open the lid and give the Pongal a good mix.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.
