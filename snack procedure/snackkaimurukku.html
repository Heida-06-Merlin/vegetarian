Kai Murukku

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1/2 cup urad dal flour
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil, for deep frying

Procedure

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, urad dal flour, cumin seeds, sesame seeds, asafoetida, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a smooth dough. The dough should be firm yet pliable.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
  4. Fill a murukku press with the dough and press it directly into the hot oil in a circular motion to form spiral shapes.
  5. Fry the murukku until golden brown and crispy. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining dough.
  7. Once cooled, store the Kai Murukku in an airtight container.
  8. Serve and enjoy as a snack!
