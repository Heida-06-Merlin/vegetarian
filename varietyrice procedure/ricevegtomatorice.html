Vegetable Tomato Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Rinse basmati rice under running water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add diced carrots, bell pepper, and green peas. Cook for 3-4 minutes until vegetables are slightly tender.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add rinsed basmati rice to the pan. Stir gently to coat the rice with the spices and vegetables.
  • Pour in enough water (usually double the quantity of rice) and bring to a boil.
  • Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  • Once done, fluff the rice with a fork. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Vegetable Tomato Rice!
