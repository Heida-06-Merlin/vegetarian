Peanut Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanuts, roasted
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • For tempering:
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon urad dal
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 curry leaves
  • 2-3 dried red chilies

Procedure

  • In a blender, add roasted peanuts, green chilies, ginger, garlic, tamarind paste, grated coconut (if using), and salt.
  • Add water gradually and blend until you get a smooth chutney consistency.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • For tempering, heat oil in a small pan.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Then add urad dal and fry until golden brown.
  • Add a pinch of asafoetida (hing), curry leaves, and dried red chilies. Fry for a few seconds.
  • Pour the tempering over the peanut chutney.
  • Mix well and serve with dosa, idli, or any South Indian snack of your choice.
