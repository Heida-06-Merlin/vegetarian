Ragi Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ragi (finger millet) flour
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (black gram lentils), soaked for 4-6 hours
  • 1/4 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Procedure

  • Drain the soaked urad dal and grind it into a smooth batter along with fenugreek seeds. Add water as needed.
  • In a separate bowl, wash and soak poha for about 15 minutes.
  • In another bowl, mix together ragi flour, rice flour, and soaked poha. Add the ground urad dal batter and mix well to combine.
  • Add salt to taste and mix the batter thoroughly. Add water gradually to achieve a dosa batter consistency.
  • Cover the batter and let it ferment overnight or for 6-8 hours in a warm place.
  • Heat a dosa tawa or griddle over medium heat.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot tawa and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  • Drizzle some oil around the edges of the dosa.
  • Cook the dosa until it turns golden brown and crisp on the bottom.
  • Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it is cooked through.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • Serve hot with chutney, sambar, or any side dish of your choice.
