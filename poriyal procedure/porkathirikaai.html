Kathirikai Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 500g Kathirikai (Indian eggplant), diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped onion, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add diced Kathirikai and mix well.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix until the spices coat the Kathirikai evenly.
  • Cover and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the Kathirikai is tender.
  • If using grated coconut, add it now and mix well.
  • Cook for another 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat.
  • Serve hot with rice or as a side dish with your favorite South Indian meal.
