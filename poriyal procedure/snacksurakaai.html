Surakaai Kootu

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized surakaai (bottle gourd), peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup split yellow lentils (moong dal)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Procedure

  • Pressure cook the split yellow lentils (moong dal) with turmeric powder until soft and mushy. Set aside.
  • In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add cumin seeds, dry red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  • Add diced surakaai (bottle gourd) to the pan. Cook until they are soft and tender.
  • Mash the cooked lentils (moong dal) and add them to the pan. Mix well.
  • Add salt to taste and grated coconut (optional). Mix well and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  • Remove from heat and transfer to a serving dish.
  • Serve hot with rice or roti. Enjoy your delicious Surakaai Kootu!
