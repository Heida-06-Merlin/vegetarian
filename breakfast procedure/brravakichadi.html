Rava Kichadi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • A few curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Procedure

  • Dry roast the semolina in a pan over low heat until it turns aromatic and light golden brown. Set aside.
  • In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, green chili, and grated ginger. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped vegetables and curry leaves. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Add water, turmeric powder, and salt. Bring it to a boil.
  • Reduce the heat to low and slowly add the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to prevent lumps from forming.
  • Cook for a few minutes until the rava kichadi thickens and the water is absorbed.
  • Remove from heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges and coconut chutney.
