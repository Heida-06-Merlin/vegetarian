Poondu Sadam

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 10-12 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon grated coconut (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat sesame oil in a pan on medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until mustard seeds start to splutter.
  • Add finely chopped garlic and sauté until they turn golden brown and fragrant.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice and gently mix until the spices coat the rice evenly.
  • Optional: Add grated coconut and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy the flavorful Poondu Sadam!
