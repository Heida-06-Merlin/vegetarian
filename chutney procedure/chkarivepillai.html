Karivepillai Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add urad dal, chana dal, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and dry red chilies. Sauté until dals turn golden brown.
  • Add minced garlic and sauté for a minute.
  • Add green chilies and curry leaves. Sauté until curry leaves turn crispy.
  • Add grated coconut and tamarind paste. Sauté for another minute.
  • Turn off the heat and allow the mixture to cool down.
  • Transfer the mixture to a blender, add salt, and grind into a smooth paste, adding water as needed.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Serve Karivepillai Chutney with dosa, idli, or rice.
