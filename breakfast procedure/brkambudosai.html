Kambu Dosai (Pearl Millet Dosai)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kambu (pearl millet) flour
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1/4 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Procedure

  • Wash and soak urad dal, rice, poha, and fenugreek seeds together for 4-6 hours.
  • Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter, adding water as needed. The batter should be of pouring consistency.
  • In a separate bowl, mix kambu flour with water to form a smooth paste.
  • Combine the kambu flour paste with the ground batter. Mix well.
  • Add salt to the batter and mix thoroughly.
  • Cover the batter and let it ferment overnight or for 6-8 hours in a warm place.
  • Heat a dosa tawa or griddle over medium heat. Grease it lightly with oil.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot tawa and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
  • Drizzle some oil around the edges and cook until the bottom surface turns golden brown.
  • Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it is cooked through.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter to make more dosas.
  • Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
