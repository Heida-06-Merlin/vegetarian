Potato Chips

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional seasoning:
    • Paprika
    • Garlic powder
    • Onion powder
    • Black pepper
    • Herbs (such as rosemary or thyme)

Procedure

  • Peel the potatoes and slice them thinly using a mandoline slicer or a sharp knife.
  • Soak the potato slices in cold water for about 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  • Drain the potato slices and pat them dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  • Working in batches, carefully add the potato slices to the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes.
  • Use a slotted spoon to transfer the fried potato chips to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil.
  • Season the potato chips with salt and any optional seasoning of your choice while they are still warm.
  • Allow the potato chips to cool completely before serving.
  • Store any leftover potato chips in an airtight container to maintain their crispiness.
