Appalam Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 4-5 appalams (papadums)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 dry red chilies, broken into pieces
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Roast the appalams (papadums) over an open flame or in a microwave until they are crisp. Break them into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add broken dry red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  • Add the roasted appalam (papadum) pieces and toss gently until they are coated with the tempering.
  • Season with salt to taste.
  • If using grated coconut, sprinkle it over the appalam poriyal and mix gently.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot as a crunchy side dish with rice or enjoy as a snack.
