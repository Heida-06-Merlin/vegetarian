Potato Red Chips

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, washed and peeled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Cut the potatoes into thin slices, about 1/8 inch thick, using a sharp knife or a mandoline slicer.
  • In a large bowl, toss the potato slices with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper until evenly coated.
  • Arrange the seasoned potato slices in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, flipping the chips halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove from the oven and let the chips cool slightly on the baking sheet.
  • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
  • Serve the potato red chips hot as a delicious snack or side dish.
