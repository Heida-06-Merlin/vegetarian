Aloo Dum Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked
  • 3-4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and halved
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup fried onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp biryani masala powder
  • Whole spices:
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 4-5 cloves
    • 2-3 green cardamoms
    • 1-inch cinnamon stick
    • 1 star anise
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or oil

Procedure

  • Parboil the potatoes until they are slightly tender. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add the whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and biryani masala powder. Mix well.
  • Add yogurt, fried onions, coriander leaves, mint leaves, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  • In a separate pot, layer soaked rice at the bottom. Top with the prepared masala mixture.
  • Add parboiled potatoes and green peas on top of the masala.
  • Add water (1:2 ratio of rice to water). Cover and cook until the rice is fully done and the potatoes are soft.
  • Gently fluff the rice and masala layers. Serve hot with raita and enjoy your delicious Aloo Dum Biryani!
