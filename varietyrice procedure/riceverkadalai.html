Verkadalai Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 1/2 cup peanuts, roasted and coarsely ground
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until raw smell disappears.
  • Add turmeric powder and roasted peanut powder. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice and salt. Mix until everything is well combined.
  • Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Verkadalai Rice!
