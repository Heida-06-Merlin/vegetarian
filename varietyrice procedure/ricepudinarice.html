Pudina Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves (pudina)
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Whole spices:
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 2 cloves
    • 2 cardamom pods
    • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  • In a blender, combine mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chilies, garlic, and ginger. Blend into a smooth paste with a little water.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add whole spices and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Stir in the mint-coriander paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  • Add drained rice to the pan and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  • Transfer everything to a rice cooker or a pot. Add salt and enough water (about 3.5 cups) to cook the rice.
  • Cook until the rice is done and fluff it with a fork.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your flavorful Pudina Rice!
