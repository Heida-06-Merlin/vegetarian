Keerai Pappu (Spinach Dal)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 2 cups chopped spinach (any variety of spinach)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash toor dal and pressure cook with turmeric powder and enough water until soft and mushy.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add crushed garlic, chopped onions, and slit green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add chopped spinach and sauté until it wilts.
  • Mash the cooked dal and add it to the pan. Mix well.
  • Add salt and adjust consistency by adding water if needed.
  • Let it simmer for a few minutes until the flavors blend well.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
