Thengai Paal Sodhi Kuzhambu (Coconut Milk Sodhi Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato, etc.), diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup thin coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and slit green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add diced mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  • Add thin coconut milk and salt to taste. Cover and cook until vegetables are tender.
  • Once the vegetables are cooked, add thick coconut milk. Stir gently and simmer for a few minutes.
  • Do not let it boil after adding thick coconut milk.
  • Switch off the heat. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Thengai Paal Sodhi Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
