Puttu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • In a bowl, mix rice flour with salt and enough water to form a crumbly mixture.
  • Take a puttu kutti (cylindrical vessel) and fill the bottom with grated coconut.
  • Layer the rice flour mixture on top of the grated coconut, alternating with layers of grated coconut until the kutti is filled, ending with a layer of grated coconut on top.
  • Place the kutti on top of a vessel with boiling water, ensuring that the water does not touch the puttu mixture.
  • Cover the top of the puttu kutti with a lid and steam for about 10-15 minutes, until the puttu is cooked and firm.
  • Remove the puttu from the kutti and serve hot with kadala curry, banana, or sugar.
