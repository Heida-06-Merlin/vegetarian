Pathiya Kuzhambu

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup cooked and mashed toor dal (pigeon pea)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon ghee or oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, heat ghee or oil over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add turmeric powder and saut√© for a few seconds.
  • Add cooked rice and mashed toor dal. Mix well.
  • Gradually add water while stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
  • Season with salt and bring the kuzhambu to a gentle boil.
  • Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the kuzhambu thickens slightly.
  • Remove from heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Pathiya Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot.
