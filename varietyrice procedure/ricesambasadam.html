Samba Sadam

Ingredients

  • 1 cup samba rice (or any short-grain rice)
  • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • A handful of curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups water

Procedure

  • Wash the rice and toor dal together and soak in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and asafoetida. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
  • Add the soaked rice and dal mixture. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  • Season with salt and add water. Stir well.
  • Close the pressure cooker lid and cook for 3 whistles on medium heat.
  • Once the pressure releases naturally, open the cooker, fluff the samba sadam gently, and serve hot with a side of pickle or yogurt.
