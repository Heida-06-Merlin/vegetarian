Beetroot Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized beetroot, peeled and grated
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 2-3 dry red chilies, broken into pieces
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add broken red chilies and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add grated beetroot and turmeric powder. Mix well.
  • Add salt to taste. Stir well and cover the pan.
  • Cook on low heat for about 8-10 minutes or until the beetroot is tender, stirring occasionally.
  • If using grated coconut, add it at this stage and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot as a side dish with rice or roti.
