Pine Pepper Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add minced garlic, minced ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add diced bell pepper and cook for a few minutes until slightly tender.
  • Add pineapple chunks and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Add soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice and toss until all ingredients are well combined.
  • Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your Pine Pepper Rice!
