Semiya Kichadi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vermicelli (semiya)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers (optional)
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • A few curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Procedure

  • Dry roast the vermicelli in a pan over medium heat until it turns light golden brown. Set aside.
  • In a separate pan, heat oil over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, green chili, grated ginger, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped vegetables and sauté for a few minutes until they are slightly cooked.
  • Add turmeric powder, asafoetida, and salt. Mix well.
  • Pour water into the pan and bring it to a boil.
  • Add roasted vermicelli to the boiling water and mix well.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for about 5-7 minutes or until the vermicelli is cooked and water is absorbed.
  • Once cooked, garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges.
