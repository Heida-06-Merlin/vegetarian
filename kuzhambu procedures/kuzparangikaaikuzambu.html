Parangikai Kuzhambu (Pumpkin Curry)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin (parangikai), diced
  • 1/2 cup tamarind extract
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onion and slit green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add diced pumpkin (parangikai) and cook for a few minutes.
  • Add tamarind extract, turmeric powder, sambar powder, asafoetida, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  • Add water if needed to adjust the consistency. Cover and cook until pumpkin is tender.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Parangikai Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
