Upma

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup green peas (optional)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (bell peppers, beans, etc.)
  • A few curry leaves
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Procedure

  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped green chilies, grated ginger, and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add chopped vegetables (carrot, peas, bell peppers, etc.) and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Meanwhile, roast the semolina in a separate pan until it turns golden brown and aromatic.
  • Add roasted semolina to the pan with vegetables. Mix well.
  • Pour water slowly while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  • Add salt and asafoetida. Mix well and cover the pan.
  • Cook on low heat for 5-7 minutes until the upma is cooked and all the water is absorbed.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges.
