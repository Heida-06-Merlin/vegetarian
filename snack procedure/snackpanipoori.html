Pani Puri

Ingredients

  • 20 puris (puri shells)
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/4 cup green chutney
  • 1/4 cup sev (crunchy chickpea flour noodles)
  • 1/4 cup boondi (fried gram flour balls)

Procedure

  • Make a hole in the center of each puri shell.
  • Fill each puri with a small amount of mashed potatoes, chickpeas, chopped onions, and tomatoes.
  • Add a small spoonful of tamarind chutney and green chutney to each puri.
  • Sprinkle chopped coriander leaves, sev, and boondi over the filled puris.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Pani Puri!
