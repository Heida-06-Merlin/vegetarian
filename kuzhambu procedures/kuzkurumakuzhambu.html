Kuruma Kuzhambu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato), diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup coconut, grated
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds (khus khus)
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds (saunf)
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (black gram)
  • 4-5 dry red chilies
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 4-5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Grind coconut, poppy seeds, fennel seeds, coriander seeds, chana dal, urad dal, dry red chilies, ginger, and garlic into a smooth paste with some water.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  • Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  • Add the ground paste, turmeric powder, salt, and water as needed. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook until the vegetables are tender and the gravy thickens.
  • Adjust salt if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Kuruma Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice or chapati.
