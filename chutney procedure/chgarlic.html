Garlic Chutney

Ingredients

  • 10-12 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 4-5 dried red chilies (adjust according to spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind pulp
  • 1 tablespoon roasted peanuts
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add garlic cloves and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Remove the garlic cloves from the pan and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add dried red chilies, grated coconut, roasted peanuts, sesame seeds, and cumin seeds.
  • Roast the ingredients until the coconut turns golden brown and aromatic.
  • Transfer the roasted ingredients to a blender or food processor.
  • Add tamarind pulp and salt to the blender.
  • Add the sautéed garlic cloves to the blender.
  • Blend all the ingredients together until you get a coarse or smooth chutney consistency, as per your preference.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Your flavorful Garlic Chutney is ready to be served!
