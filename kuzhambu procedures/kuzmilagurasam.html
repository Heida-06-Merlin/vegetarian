Milagu Rasam (Pepper Rasam)

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 tablespoon black peppercorns
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil

Procedure

  • Cook toor dal in a pressure cooker until soft. Mash and set aside.
  • In a pot, add chopped tomatoes, slit green chilies, turmeric powder, salt, and water. Bring to a boil and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  • Add cooked toor dal (pigeon peas) to the pot. Mix well.
  • In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add black peppercorns to the pan and sauté for a minute until aromatic.
  • Grind the sautéed peppercorns into a coarse powder.
  • Add the ground pepper powder to the pot with the tomato and dal mixture. Mix well.
  • Bring the rasam to a gentle boil. Remove from heat.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Milagu Rasam is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice or enjoy as a soup.
