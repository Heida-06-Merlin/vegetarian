Ennai Kathirikai Kuzhambu (Stuffed Brinjal Curry)

Ingredients

  • 6-8 small brinjals (kathirikai)
  • 1/2 cup tamarind extract
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash and slit the brinjals, keeping the stem intact.
  • Heat sesame oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, crushed garlic, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add sambar powder, turmeric powder, and asafoetida. Mix well.
  • Stuff the prepared masala mixture into the slit brinjals.
  • Heat sesame oil in a pan. Add stuffed brinjals and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Add tamarind extract, salt, and water (if needed). Bring it to a boil.
  • Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the brinjals are cooked and the kuzhambu thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
