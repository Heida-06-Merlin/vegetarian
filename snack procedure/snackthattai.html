Thattai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 2 tablespoons urad dal flour (black gram flour)
  • 2 tablespoons roasted gram flour (chickpea flour)
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon white sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon chopped curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, urad dal flour, roasted gram flour, grated coconut, sesame seeds, chopped curry leaves, red chili powder, asafoetida, and salt.
  • Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a smooth dough. The dough should be firm and not too sticky.
  • Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them into thin discs using your palms or a rolling pin.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Gently slide the flattened discs into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  • Remove the fried thattai onto a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  • Allow them to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
  • Serve and enjoy crunchy Thattai as a snack!
