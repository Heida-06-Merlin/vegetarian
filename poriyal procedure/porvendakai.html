Vendakkai Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 250g okra (vendakkai), washed, dried, and sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and urad dal. Let them splutter and turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped onions, slit green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add sliced okra, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the okra is tender.
  • If desired, add grated coconut and mix well.
  • Adjust salt to taste.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish with rice or chapati.
