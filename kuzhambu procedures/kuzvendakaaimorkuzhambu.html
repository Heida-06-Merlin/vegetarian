Vendakkai Mor Kuzhambu (Okra Buttermilk Curry)

Ingredients

  • 10-12 okra (vendakkai), chopped into small pieces
  • 1 cup sour curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Whisk sour curd (yogurt) with turmeric powder and salt.
  • Grind grated coconut, green chilies, and cumin seeds into a smooth paste using a little water.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped okra (vendakkai) to the pan and saut√© until they are cooked and slightly crispy.
  • Add the ground coconut paste to the pan and mix well.
  • Reduce the heat to low and add the whisked curd mixture to the pan. Stir continuously to avoid curdling.
  • Let the kuzhambu simmer for 5-7 minutes until flavors are well combined.
  • Adjust salt if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Vendakkai Mor Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
