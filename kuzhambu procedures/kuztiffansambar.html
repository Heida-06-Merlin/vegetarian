Tiffin Sambar

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato), chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Pressure cook toor dal with enough water until soft and mushy.
  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, slit green chilies, and mixed vegetables. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  • Add turmeric powder, sambar powder, asafoetida, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked toor dal and water to adjust the consistency. Bring it to a boil.
  • Simmer for 5-10 minutes until the flavors blend well and the sambar thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with idli, dosa, or vada.
