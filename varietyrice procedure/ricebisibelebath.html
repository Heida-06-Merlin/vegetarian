Bisi Bele Bath

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons bisi bele bath powder*
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon jaggery or sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4-5 dry red chilies
  • A few curry leaves
  • 3-4 cups water
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash rice and toor dal together, then soak for 30 minutes.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until fragrant.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  • Drain the soaked rice and dal and add them to the cooker.
  • Add bisi bele bath powder, tamarind paste, jaggery or sugar, salt, and water. Mix well.
  • Pressure cook for 3-4 whistles or until rice and dal are cooked thoroughly.
  • Once the pressure releases, open the cooker, give it a good mix, and check the consistency. Adjust water if needed.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with a dollop of ghee and enjoy the delicious Bisi Bele Bath!
