Idli

Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice (parboiled rice)
  • 1 cup urad dal (black lentils)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Wash the idli rice and urad dal separately a few times until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water separately for 4-6 hours.
  3. Grind the soaked urad dal and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter using a wet grinder or blender. Transfer to a large bowl.
  4. Grind the soaked idli rice into a slightly coarse batter. Mix it with the urad dal batter in the bowl.
  5. Add salt to the batter and mix well. The batter should be thick but pourable. Cover and ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or until it doubles in volume.
  6. Grease the idli moulds with oil or ghee. Pour the batter into the moulds, filling them to about 3/4 full.
  7. Steam the idlis in a steamer for 10-12 minutes until they are cooked through. Insert a toothpick into the center of an idli to check for doneness. It should come out clean.
  8. Remove the idli mould from the steamer and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Use a spoon or knife to gently remove the idlis from the moulds.
  10. Serve hot idlis with sambar, coconut chutney, or any other side dish of your choice.
