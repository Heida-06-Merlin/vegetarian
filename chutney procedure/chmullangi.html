Mullangi Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized radish (mullangi), peeled and grated
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add chopped onion and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add grated radish and cook until it becomes soft.
  • Add grated coconut and tamarind paste. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and let it cool.
  • Transfer the mixture to a blender or food processor. Add salt to taste.
  • Blend into a smooth chutney consistency.
  • Transfer to a serving bowl and serve with dosa, idli, or any South Indian breakfast dish.
