Poricha Kuzhambu (Spiced Lentil Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1/2 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 small brinjals (eggplants), chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 6-8 beans, chopped
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 drumstick, cut into pieces
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Pressure cook toor dal and chana dal until soft. Mash them well and set aside.
  • Dry roast grated coconut, dry red chilies, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, fenugreek seeds, and black peppercorns until fragrant. Grind into a fine paste.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped vegetables (brinjals, carrot, beans, potato, drumstick) and saut√© for a few minutes.
  • Add chopped tomato and cook until it turns soft.
  • Add the ground spice paste, turmeric powder, asafoetida, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  • Add the mashed dal mixture to the pan. Mix well and add water to adjust the consistency.
  • Bring the kuzhambu to a boil and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are cooked through.
  • Adjust salt if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Poricha Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
