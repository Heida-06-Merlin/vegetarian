Thirunelveli Sodhi Kuzhambu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato, etc.), chopped
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash the moong dal and pressure cook with chopped vegetables, slit green chilies, turmeric powder, and salt until soft.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add the cooked dal and vegetables to the pan along with thick coconut milk. Mix well.
  • Simmer the kuzhambu on low heat for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until flavors are well combined.
  • Adjust salt if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Thirunelveli Sodhi Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
