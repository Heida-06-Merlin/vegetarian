Puliyodharai

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 1/4 cup tamarind paste
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Jaggery or sugar to taste (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chana dal, urad dal, peanuts, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until dals turn golden brown.
  • Add turmeric powder and asafoetida, mix well.
  • Add tamarind paste and cook until the raw smell disappears and oil separates.
  • Add salt and jaggery/sugar (if using), mix well.
  • Add cooked rice to the pan and gently mix until the rice is well coated with the tamarind mixture.
  • Turn off the heat and let the flavors meld for a few minutes.
  • Serve hot or at room temperature with papad and yogurt.
