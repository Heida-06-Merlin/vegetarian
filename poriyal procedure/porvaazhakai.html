Vaazhakai Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 2 raw bananas (vaazhakai), peeled and chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and urad dal. Let them splutter and turn golden brown.
  • Add slit green chilies, curry leaves, and chopped onions. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped raw bananas, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well to coat the bananas with spices.
  • Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the bananas are tender but not mushy.
  • Once the bananas are cooked, remove the lid and cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow excess moisture to evaporate.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot as a side dish with rice or roti.
