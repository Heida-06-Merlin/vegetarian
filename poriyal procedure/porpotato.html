Potato Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan or kadai over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Fry until they turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add diced potatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook on low heat until potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally.
  • Once potatoes are cooked, remove the lid and increase the heat to medium-high. Cook until potatoes are slightly crispy and golden brown.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with rice or as a side dish with any South Indian meal.
