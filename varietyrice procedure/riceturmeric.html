Turmeric Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: chopped cilantro for garnish

Procedure

  • Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  • In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  • Once the water is boiling, add the rinsed rice, turmeric powder, vegetable oil or ghee, and salt to taste.
  • Stir well and reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and all the water is absorbed.
  • Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  • Fluff the rice with a fork before serving. Garnish with chopped cilantro if desired.
