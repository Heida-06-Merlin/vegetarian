Beetroot Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 2 medium-sized beetroot, grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them crackle.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add grated beetroot and sauté until it is cooked and the raw smell disappears.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice and gently mix until the beetroot mixture is evenly distributed.
  • Cover and cook for a few minutes on low heat, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your nutritious Beetroot Rice!
