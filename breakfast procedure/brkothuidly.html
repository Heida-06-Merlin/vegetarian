Kothu Idly

Ingredients

  • 4-5 leftover idlis, diced into small pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped cabbage
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onion and saut√© until it turns translucent.
  • Add chopped green chilies, chopped tomato, grated carrot, chopped bell pepper, green peas, and chopped cabbage. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  • Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  • Add diced idli pieces and mix everything together until well combined. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the idli pieces are heated through and start to turn golden brown.
  • Remove from heat. Optionally, sprinkle lemon juice over the kothu idly and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
