Karuvepillai Kuzhambu (Curry Leaves Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon-sized tamarind ball
  • 1 cup fresh curry leaves, tightly packed
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon jaggery or brown sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Soak tamarind in warm water and extract the juice.
  • Dry roast urad dal, chana dal, dry red chilies, and fenugreek seeds until fragrant. Grind into a fine paste along with grated coconut.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and sauté until they turn mushy.
  • Add curry leaves and sauté until they wilt.
  • Add tamarind extract, turmeric powder, asafoetida, jaggery or brown sugar (if using), and salt to taste. Mix well.
  • Add ground spice paste to the pan. Mix well and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Adjust salt and sweetness if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Karuvepillai Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
