Karupatti Mittai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 cup grated palm jaggery (karupatti)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of salt

Procedure

  1. In a saucepan, combine grated palm jaggery and water. Heat it over medium heat until the jaggery completely dissolves to form a syrup. Strain the syrup to remove any impurities.
  2. Transfer the strained syrup back to the saucepan. Add cardamom powder and a pinch of salt. Stir well.
  3. Bring the syrup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.
  4. Gradually add rice flour to the syrup while continuously stirring to avoid lumps. Keep stirring until the mixture thickens and forms a smooth dough-like consistency.
  5. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool slightly.
  6. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls or any desired shapes. You can also flatten them slightly to make patties.
  7. Optional: Garnish the karupatti mittai with grated coconut.
  8. Let the karupatti mittai cool completely before serving. Enjoy this traditional Indian sweet!
