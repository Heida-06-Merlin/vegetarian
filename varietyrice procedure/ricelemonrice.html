Lemon Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, roasted
  • 1/4 cup cashews, roasted
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 lemon, juice extracted
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Now add cumin seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a minute.
  • Add roasted peanuts and cashews, mix well.
  • Lower the heat, add turmeric powder, and mix quickly.
  • Now add cooked rice, salt, and lemon juice. Mix everything gently until well combined.
  • Cover and let it cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat.
  • Turn off the heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  • Your delicious Lemon Rice is ready to be served!
