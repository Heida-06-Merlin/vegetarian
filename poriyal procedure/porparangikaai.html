Parangikai Kootu

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced parangikai (pumpkin)
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 1 tablespoon grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or any cooking oil
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Few curry leaves

Procedure

  • Pressure cook moong dal with diced parangikai, turmeric powder, and salt until soft and mushy.
  • Heat coconut oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until fragrant.
  • Add the cooked dal-parangikai mixture to the pan and mix well.
  • Add grated coconut and mix again.
  • Simmer for a few minutes until everything is well combined and the flavors are infused.
  • Adjust salt if needed. Remove from heat.
  • Parangikai kootu is ready to be served.
  • Enjoy hot with steamed rice or as a side dish!
