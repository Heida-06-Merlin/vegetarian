Pachai Milagai Thogayal

Ingredients

  • 10-12 green chilies
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons urad dal
  • 2 tablespoons chana dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add urad dal, chana dal, and green chilies. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown.
  • Remove the mixture from the pan and let it cool.
  • In the same pan, add the remaining 1 tablespoon oil.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add curry leaves and sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add grated coconut and sauté until it turns golden brown.
  • Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool.
  • In a blender, combine the cooled dal mixture, coconut mixture, tamarind paste, asafoetida, and salt.
  • Blend into a coarse paste without adding water.
  • Your Pachai Milagai Thogayal is ready to serve!
  • Enjoy it as a condiment with rice or dosa.
