Vellarikaai Thuvaiyal

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized ridge gourd (vellarikaai)
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Peel the ridge gourd, remove seeds if they are hard, and chop it into small pieces.
  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add urad dal, chana dal, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped ridge gourd pieces and sauté until they are cooked and tender.
  • Once cooked, allow the mixture to cool down to room temperature.
  • In a blender or food processor, add the cooked ridge gourd mixture, grated coconut, green chilies, ginger, and salt. Blend until you get a smooth paste.
  • Transfer the thuvaiyal to a serving bowl.
  • Your delicious Vellarikaai Thuvaiyal is ready to be served with steamed rice or dosa.
