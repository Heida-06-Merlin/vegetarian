Mint Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh mint leaves, washed and stemmed
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves, washed and stemmed
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Procedure

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine mint leaves, cilantro leaves, green chilies, ginger, garlic, lemon juice, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add a little water to help with blending, and blend until you achieve a smooth consistency.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
  4. Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  5. Serve fresh with your favorite snacks or dishes.
