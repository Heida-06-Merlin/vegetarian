Rava Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rava (semolina)
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • A few curry leaves, finely chopped
  • A handful of coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon black peppercorns, crushed
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rava, rice flour, all-purpose flour, chopped onion, chopped green chili, grated ginger, chopped curry leaves, chopped coriander leaves, cumin seeds, crushed black peppercorns, and salt.
  • Add water gradually and mix well to form a smooth batter. The batter should be thin and pourable. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  • Heat a dosa tawa or griddle over medium heat.
  • Stir the batter well and pour it onto the hot tawa in a circular motion from the outer edges to the center to form a thin dosa.
  • Drizzle some oil around the edges of the dosa.
  • Cook the dosa until it turns golden brown and crisp on the bottom.
  • Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it is cooked through.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • Serve hot with chutney, sambar, or any side dish of your choice.
