Methi Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup fresh methi (fenugreek) leaves, washed and chopped
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  • Add turmeric powder and garam masala. Mix well.
  • Add chopped methi leaves and sauté until they wilt.
  • Add drained basmati rice. Mix gently until rice is coated with the masala.
  • Add 4 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook until the rice is done and water is absorbed.
  • Fluff the rice gently with a fork. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with raita or your favorite side dish.
