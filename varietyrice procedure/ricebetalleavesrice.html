Betel Leaves Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 10-12 betel leaves (paan)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash the betel leaves and chop them finely.
  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped betel leaves and cook until they wilt.
  • Add turmeric powder, grated coconut, and roasted peanuts. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice and salt. Mix until everything is well combined and heated through.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your flavorful Betel Leaves Rice!
