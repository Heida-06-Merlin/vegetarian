Instant Pot Sambar

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato), chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup tamarind extract
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Wash toor dal and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  • Heat oil in the Instant Pot using the "Sauté" mode.
  • Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, green chilies, and mixed vegetables. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Add soaked toor dal, tamarind extract, sambar powder, turmeric powder, asafoetida, salt, and water (enough to cover the ingredients).
  • Close the lid of the Instant Pot and set it to "Manual" mode for 8 minutes on high pressure.
  • Once done, let the pressure release naturally for a few minutes and then do a quick release.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or idli.
