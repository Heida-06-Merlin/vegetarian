Onion Uttapam

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Procedure

  • Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle over medium heat.
  • Spread a ladleful of dosa batter on the hot tawa to form a thick round pancake.
  • Sprinkle chopped onions, green chili, and coriander leaves evenly on the dosa.
  • Drizzle some oil or ghee around the edges and on top of the dosa.
  • Cook the uttapam on medium heat until the bottom side turns golden brown and crispy.
  • Flip the uttapam and cook the other side until it is cooked through and crispy.
  • Remove the uttapam from the tawa and serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
