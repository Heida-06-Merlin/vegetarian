Aama Vadai (Paruppu Vadai)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 1 inch ginger, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh curry leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • Soak chana dal in water for 2-3 hours. Drain well.
  • In a blender or food processor, coarsely grind chana dal along with dry red chilies, ginger, and garlic without adding water.
  • Transfer the ground mixture to a mixing bowl.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, curry leaves, fennel seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and salt. Mix well.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadhai.
  • Take a small portion of the mixture, shape it into a round ball, and flatten it slightly to form a vadai.
  • Gently slide the vadai into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  • Remove the vadai from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Serve hot with coconut chutney or tomato chutney.
