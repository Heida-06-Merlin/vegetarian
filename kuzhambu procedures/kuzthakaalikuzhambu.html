Thakaali Sambaar (Tomato Sambhar)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Cook toor dal in a pressure cooker until soft. Mash and set aside.
  • Soak tamarind in warm water and extract the juice.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  • Add turmeric powder, sambar powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add tamarind extract and bring it to a boil.
  • Add cooked toor dal and water to adjust the consistency. Mix well.
  • Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sambhar thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Thakaali Sambaar is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice or idli.
