Ellu Urundai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sesame seeds (ellu)
  • 1 cup grated jaggery (vellam)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee

Procedure

  1. Heat a pan and dry roast sesame seeds on medium flame until they turn golden brown and release a nutty aroma. Set aside to cool.
  2. In the same pan, add grated jaggery and ghee. Heat on low flame until the jaggery melts completely.
  3. Once the jaggery melts, turn off the heat and add the roasted sesame seeds. Mix well until the sesame seeds are evenly coated with the jaggery mixture.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool slightly until it's warm enough to handle but not too hot.
  5. Grease your palms with a little ghee and take small portions of the mixture. Roll them into round balls or desired shapes while the mixture is still warm.
  6. Repeat the process until all the mixture is used up.
  7. Let the ellu urundai cool completely before storing them in an airtight container.
  8. Enjoy these delicious and nutritious sesame seed balls as a snack!
