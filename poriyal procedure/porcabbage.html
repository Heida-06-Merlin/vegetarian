Cabbage Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add shredded cabbage, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook for about 5-7 minutes or until the cabbage is tender, stirring occasionally.
  • Once the cabbage is cooked, add curry leaves and grated coconut (if using). Mix well and cook for another minute.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish with rice or roti.
