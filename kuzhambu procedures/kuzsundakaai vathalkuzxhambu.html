Sundakaai Vathal Kuzhambu (Turkey Berry Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sundakaai vathal (dried turkey berries)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tamarind extract
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Soak sundakaai vathal in warm water for 30 minutes.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add turmeric powder, sambar powder, asafoetida, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add soaked sundakaai vathal and tamarind extract. Bring it to a boil.
  • Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the vathal is cooked and the gravy thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
