Ginger Chutney

Ingredients

  • 100g fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon jaggery or brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  • Add chopped ginger, green chilies, onion, and garlic. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add turmeric powder and sauté for another minute.
  • Add tamarind paste, jaggery (or brown sugar), and salt. Mix well.
  • Cook for 5-7 minutes until the ginger softens and the mixture thickens.
  • Remove from heat and let it cool.
  • Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend until smooth.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  • Serve with your favorite snacks or as a condiment.
