Kondakadalai Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (kondakadalai), drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chili (if using). Sauté for another minute.
  • Add diced tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  • Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  • Add drained chickpeas (kondakadalai) and salt to taste. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Add cooked rice to the pan and gently mix until well combined.
  • Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot.
