Special Mixture

Ingredients

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 cup thin sev
  • 1 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/2 cup roasted chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 cup roasted cashews
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup dried coconut slices
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the puffed rice, thin sev, roasted peanuts, roasted chana dal, roasted cashews, raisins, and dried coconut slices.
  • Heat oil in a small pan over medium heat.
  • Add chaat masala, red chili powder, black salt, cumin powder, turmeric powder, and asafoetida to the hot oil. Stir quickly for about 30 seconds to release the flavors.
  • Pour the spice mixture over the dry ingredients in the mixing bowl. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly coated with the spices.
  • Spread the mixture on a baking sheet or large plate and let it cool completely.
  • Store the special mixture in an airtight container and enjoy as a tasty snack!
