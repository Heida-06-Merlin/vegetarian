Curd Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional, for creamier consistency)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon chopped mint leaves
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Mix cooked rice with yogurt and milk (if using) in a bowl.
  • Heat oil or ghee in a small pan, add mustard seeds, and let them crackle.
  • Add cumin seeds and asafoetida. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  • Pour this tempering over the rice and yogurt mixture.
  • Add chopped coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chili (if using), and salt. Mix well.
  • Adjust consistency by adding more yogurt or milk if needed.
  • Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  • Serve cold as a refreshing side dish or as a light meal on its own.
