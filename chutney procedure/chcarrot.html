Carrot Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 large carrots, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped ginger
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, and curry leaves. Sauté until they splutter.
  • Add chopped ginger and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add grated carrots and sauté until they turn soft.
  • Add grated coconut and tamarind paste. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Turn off the heat and allow the mixture to cool down to room temperature.
  • Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend it into a smooth paste. Add a little water if needed.
  • Season with salt according to taste.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Carrot chutney is ready to be served with dosa, idli, or any other South Indian snacks.
