Sigapu Kudai Milagai Chutney

Ingredients

  • 10-12 dry red chilies (preferably Sigapu Kudai Milagai)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small piece of tamarind (about 1 teaspoon pulp)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Water as needed

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the dry red chilies and roast them until they become slightly darkened and aromatic. Be careful not to burn them.
  • Remove the roasted chilies from the pan and set them aside to cool.
  • In the same pan, add chopped onion and garlic. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Once cooled, transfer the roasted red chilies to a blender or grinder. Add the sautéed onion, garlic, tamarind pulp, and salt.
  • Grind everything together to a smooth paste, adding water as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Your Sigapu Kudai Milagai Chutney is ready to be served with dosa, idli, or any South Indian snack!
