Bread Roast

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons butter or oil
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • In a shallow dish, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, pepper, and chili powder (if using).
  • Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  • Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring it is fully coated on both sides.
  • Place the coated bread slices in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  • Remove from the skillet and place on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
