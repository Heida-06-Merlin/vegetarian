Murukku

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup urad dal flour (black gram flour)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, urad dal flour, melted butter, cumin seeds, sesame seeds, asafoetida, red chili powder, and salt.
  • Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a smooth dough. The dough should be firm yet pliable.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Take a portion of the dough and place it inside a murukku press fitted with a star-shaped plate.
  • Press the dough directly into the hot oil in a circular motion to form spiral shapes.
  • Fry the murukku until they turn golden brown and crisp. Fry them in batches, do not overcrowd the pan.
  • Once done, remove the murukku using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Allow them to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
  • Your delicious Murukku is ready to be enjoyed as a snack!
