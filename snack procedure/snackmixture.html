Mixture

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanuts
  • 1 cup roasted chickpeas (chana)
  • 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 cup sev (crunchy chickpea flour noodles)
  • 1/2 cup roasted cashews
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup dried coconut slices
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a large pan or kadhai over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add asafoetida and curry leaves. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  • Add peanuts, roasted chickpeas, and cashews. Roast until they turn golden brown.
  • Add flattened rice (poha) and mix well. Roast for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix until everything is well combined.
  • Add sev, raisins, and dried coconut slices. Mix well and roast for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down completely.
  • Store in an airtight container and enjoy your crunchy Mixture as a snack!
