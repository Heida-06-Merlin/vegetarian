Kaara Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 small piece of tamarind
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • Curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal, chana dal, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until dals turn golden brown.
  • Add grated coconut, garlic, and tamarind. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Remove from heat and allow it to cool.
  • Transfer the mixture to a blender or grinder. Add salt to taste.
  • Grind into a coarse paste without adding water.
  • Transfer to a serving bowl.
  • Serve with dosa, idli, or any South Indian snack of your choice.
