Paneer Biryani

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices:
    • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup fried onions (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Whole spices:
    • Bay leaves
    • Cinnamon
    • Cardamom
    • Cloves

Procedure

  • Marinate paneer cubes in yogurt and spices, refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  • Boil soaked basmati rice with whole spices until partially cooked. Drain and set aside.
  • In a separate pan, heat ghee/oil and saut√© sliced onions until golden brown.
  • Add diced tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Add marinated paneer and cook until the paneer is slightly browned.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the paneer mixture.
  • Top with fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and flavors are absorbed.
  • Gently mix the layers before serving.
  • Serve hot with raita or salad.
