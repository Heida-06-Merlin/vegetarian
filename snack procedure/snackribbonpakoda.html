Ribbon Pakoda

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 2 tablespoons hot oil
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Water as needed

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, besan, moong dal, red chili powder, asafoetida, turmeric powder, and salt.
  • Add hot oil to the mixture and mix well until the oil is well incorporated.
  • Add water little by little and knead into a stiff dough.
  • Heat oil for deep frying in a kadai or deep frying pan.
  • Take a portion of the dough and place it in the murukku press fitted with a ribbon pakoda plate.
  • Press the dough in hot oil in a circular motion to form ribbon-like shapes.
  • Fry the ribbon pakodas until they turn golden brown and crisp.
  • Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining dough.
  • Once cooled, store ribbon pakodas in an airtight container.
  • Serve as a snack with tea or coffee.
