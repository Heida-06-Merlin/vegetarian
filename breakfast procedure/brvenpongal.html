Ven Pongal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup split moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 tablespoon black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup cashews
  • 1 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Wash the rice and moong dal together until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  • In a pressure cooker, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee over medium heat.
  • Add black peppercorns and cumin seeds. Sauté until fragrant.
  • Add chopped ginger, curry leaves, and cashews. Sauté until cashews turn golden brown.
  • Add the washed rice and dal mixture to the cooker. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Add water, salt, and asafoetida. Mix well.
  • Cover the pressure cooker with its lid and cook on high heat until 2 whistles.
  • Reduce the heat to low and cook for another 10-15 minutes.
  • Allow the pressure to release naturally before opening the cooker.
  • Open the cooker and mix the pongal well. If it's too thick, you can add a little hot water to adjust the consistency.
  • Heat the remaining ghee in a small pan. Pour it over the pongal and mix well.
  • Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
