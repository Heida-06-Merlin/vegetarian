Pachai Mor Kuzhambu (Green Buttermilk Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sour curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Grind grated coconut, green chilies, and cumin seeds into a smooth paste using a little water.
  • Whisk sour curd (yogurt) with turmeric powder and salt.
  • Add the ground coconut paste to the whisked curd and mix well.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add curry leaves and then pour the curd mixture into the pan.
  • Keep stirring continuously on low heat until the mixture comes to a gentle boil. Be careful not to boil vigorously as the curd may split.
  • Once it starts boiling, remove from heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with rice.
