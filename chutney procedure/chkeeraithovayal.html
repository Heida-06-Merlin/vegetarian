Keerai Thovaiyal

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped keerai (spinach, amaranth, or any greens of your choice)
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons urad dal (black gram dal)
  • 2 tablespoons grated ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A few curry leaves

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add urad dal and roast until it turns golden brown.
  • Add chopped green chilies, grated ginger, and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped keerai (greens) and sauté until they wilt and cook down.
  • Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down.
  • Once cooled, transfer the mixture to a blender.
  • Add grated coconut, asafoetida, and salt. Blend into a coarse paste without adding water.
  • For tempering, heat a small amount of oil in a pan.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Pour the tempering over the thovaiyal and mix well.
  • Your Keerai Thovaiyal is ready to serve!
