Capsicum Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 2 large capsicums (bell peppers), diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.
  • Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chili. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add diced capsicum and cook until they are slightly tender but still crunchy.
  • Season with turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice to the pan and gently toss until well combined with the capsicum mixture. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Capsicum Rice!
