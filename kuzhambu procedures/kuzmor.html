Mor Kuzhambu (Buttermilk Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sour buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Whisk the buttermilk well and set aside.
  • In a blender, grind grated coconut, rice flour, turmeric powder, and a little water to make a smooth paste.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add the coconut paste to the pan and saut√© for a few minutes.
  • Gradually add the whisked buttermilk while stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
  • Add salt and let it simmer on low heat until the raw smell of the coconut disappears and the curry thickens.
  • Remove from heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice.
