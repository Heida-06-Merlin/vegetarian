Maangai Vathal Kuzhambu (Dried Mango Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup maangai vathal (dried mango pieces)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Soak maangai vathal (dried mango pieces) in warm water for 15-20 minutes.
  • Soak tamarind in warm water and extract the juice.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  • Add tamarind extract, sambar powder, turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  • Add soaked maangai vathal (dried mango pieces) and water to adjust the consistency. Stir well.
  • Bring the kuzhambu to a boil and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the flavors meld together.
  • Adjust salt if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Maangai Vathal Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
