Paruppu Vadai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 sprig curry leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • Wash and soak chana dal in water for 2-3 hours.
  • Drain the water completely and transfer the soaked dal to a blender.
  • Grind the dal into a coarse paste without adding water.
  • Transfer the ground dal to a mixing bowl.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, curry leaves, grated ginger, chopped red chilies, fennel seeds, cumin seeds, and salt to the dal mixture.
  • Mix everything well to form a thick batter.
  • Heat oil for deep frying in a pan over medium heat.
  • Take a small portion of the batter, shape it into a flat round vada and gently slide it into the hot oil.
  • Fry the vadas until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  • Remove the fried vadas using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • Paruppu Vadai is ready to be served hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
