Sevai (Idiyappam)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • In a large bowl, add rice flour and salt. Mix well.
  • Slowly add water while stirring continuously to form a smooth dough.
  • Heat water in a steamer or idli cooker.
  • Grease the idiyappam maker (sevai nazhi) with oil.
  • Fill the idiyappam maker with the dough.
  • Press the dough onto idli plates in a circular motion to form a coil or onto greased banana leaves or parchment paper.
  • Place the idli plates in the steamer or idli cooker and steam for 8-10 minutes until the idiyappam is cooked through.
  • Once cooked, remove the idiyappam from the steamer and let it cool slightly.
  • Serve hot with coconut milk, sweetened coconut milk, coconut chutney, or any curry of your choice.
