Thenga Bun

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Procedure

  • In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, melted butter, and coconut milk.
  • Add the yeast mixture to the flour mixture and knead until you get a smooth and elastic dough.
  • Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  • Punch down the dough and divide it into equal portions. Shape each portion into a bun.
  • Place the shaped buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, cover, and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  • Brush the risen buns with beaten egg wash and sprinkle shredded coconut on top.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the buns are golden brown and sound hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  • Remove from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack.
  • Serve and enjoy your delicious Thenga Buns!
