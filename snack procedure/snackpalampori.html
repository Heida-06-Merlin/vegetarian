Palam Pori

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons rice flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, rice flour, sugar, salt, and cardamom powder.
  • Add water gradually and mix until you get a smooth batter with a slightly thick consistency.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Dip the banana slices into the batter, making sure they are evenly coated.
  • Carefully drop the coated banana slices into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the fried banana slices using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Palam Pori!
