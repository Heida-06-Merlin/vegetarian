Carrot Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat coconut oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped green chilies and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add grated carrots and salt. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the carrots are cooked but still retain their crunch.
  • Once the carrots are cooked, add grated coconut and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish with rice or roti.
