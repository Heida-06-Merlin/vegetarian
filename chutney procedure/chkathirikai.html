Kathirikaai Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped brinjal (eggplant)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated coconut (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Fry until they turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped onions, garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add chopped brinjal, tamarind paste, and grated coconut (if using). Cook until brinjal becomes soft.
  • Turn off the heat and allow the mixture to cool down.
  • Transfer the cooled mixture to a blender and blend into a smooth paste.
  • Adjust salt as per taste.
  • For tempering, heat a little oil in a small pan. Add a pinch of asafoetida and curry leaves. Pour this tempering over the chutney.
  • Stir well and serve with dosa, idli, or rice.
