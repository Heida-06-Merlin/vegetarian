Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat ghee or oil in a large pot or pressure cooker.
  • Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and bay leaves. Sauté until fragrant.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • Add diced carrots, potatoes, cauliflower florets, green peas, and chopped green beans. Sauté for a few minutes until slightly tender.
  • Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Stir well to coat the rice with the vegetables and spices.
  • Pour in vegetable broth or water. Add salt to taste. Bring to a boil.
  • If using a pressure cooker, cover with the lid and cook for 1 whistle on medium heat. If using a pot, cover with a lid and simmer until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed, about 15-20 minutes.
  • Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Vegetable Pulao!
