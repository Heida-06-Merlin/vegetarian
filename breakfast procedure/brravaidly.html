Rava Idli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 carrot, grated (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for greasing idli molds

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, add semolina, yogurt, and water. Mix well and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  • Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, and chana dal. Let them splutter.
  • Add asafoetida, chopped green chili, grated ginger, grated carrot, and chopped coriander leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add the sautéed ingredients to the soaked semolina mixture. Mix well.
  • Add salt and baking soda. Mix until well combined. The batter should be thick yet pourable.
  • Grease the idli molds with oil. Pour the batter into each mold.
  • Steam the idlis in a steamer for about 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Once done, remove the idlis from the molds and let them cool for a minute.
  • Serve hot with chutney, sambar, or any side dish of your choice.
