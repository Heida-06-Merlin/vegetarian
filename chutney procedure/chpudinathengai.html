Pudina Thengai Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves (pudina)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut (thengai)
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (black gram)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and urad dal. Fry until the dal turns golden brown.
  • Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add fresh mint leaves and grated coconut. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the mint leaves wilt.
  • Turn off the heat and allow the mixture to cool down.
  • In a blender, add the roasted peanuts, tamarind paste, and the cooled mixture from the pan.
  • Add salt to taste. Blend everything into a smooth paste, adding water as needed.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Serve with dosa, idli, or any South Indian snack of your choice.
