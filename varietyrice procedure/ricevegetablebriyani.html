Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes, bell peppers), chopped - 2 cups
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices:
    • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Whole spices:
    • Bay leaves
    • Cinnamon
    • Cardamom
    • Cloves
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fried onions (optional)
  • Ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp biryani masala powder

Procedure

  • Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  • Add assorted vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  • In a bowl, mix yogurt with red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Add this mixture to the vegetables.
  • Cook until the vegetables are partially done.
  • In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice and cook until it is 70-80% done. Drain the rice and set aside.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the vegetable mixture.
  • Sprinkle biryani masala powder, fried onions, and chopped coriander and mint leaves on top.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  • Gently mix the layers, ensuring the vegetables and rice are evenly distributed.
  • Serve hot with raita or salad.
