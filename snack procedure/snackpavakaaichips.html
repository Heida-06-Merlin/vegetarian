Pavakkai Chips

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized bitter gourds (pavakkai)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • Wash the bitter gourds and pat them dry with a kitchen towel.
  • Trim the ends of the bitter gourds and slice them thinly using a mandoline or a sharp knife.
  • Place the sliced bitter gourds in a bowl and sprinkle salt, turmeric powder, and red chili powder over them. Toss well to coat evenly.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Once the oil is hot, carefully add the seasoned bitter gourd slices in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  • Fry the chips until they turn golden brown and crispy, stirring occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  • Using a slotted spoon, transfer the fried chips to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil.
  • Repeat the frying process with the remaining batches of seasoned bitter gourd slices.
  • Once all the chips are fried and drained, allow them to cool completely before serving.
  • Enjoy the crispy and flavorful Pavakkai Chips as a snack or accompaniment!
