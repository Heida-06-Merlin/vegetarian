Thayir Seedai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup urad dal flour
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • Oil for frying
  • Few curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 dried red chili, broken into pieces
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, urad dal flour, grated coconut, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and salt.
  • Add yogurt gradually and knead into a smooth dough. Adjust yogurt quantity as needed.
  • Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them slightly to form seedai.
  • Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  • Fry the seedai in batches on medium heat until golden brown and crispy. Drain excess oil and set aside.
  • In a separate pan, heat a little oil. Add mustard seeds, dried red chili, and curry leaves. Fry until mustard seeds crackle.
  • Pour this tempering over the fried seedai.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Thayir Seedai is ready to be served. Enjoy!
