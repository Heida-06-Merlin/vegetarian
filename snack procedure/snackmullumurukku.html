Mullu Murukku

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup urad dal flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, urad dal flour, melted butter, cumin seeds, sesame seeds, asafoetida, red chili powder, and salt.
  • Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a smooth dough. The dough should be firm.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
  • Meanwhile, fill the murukku press with a portion of the dough and fit it with the star-shaped plate.
  • Press the dough directly into the hot oil in a circular motion to form swirls or concentric circles. Make sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  • Fry the murukku until they turn golden brown and crispy, flipping them occasionally to ensure even frying.
  • Remove the fried murukku using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining dough.
  • Once cooled, store the Mullu Murukku in an airtight container. Enjoy as a crunchy snack!
