Puli Kuzhambu (Tamarind Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon-sized tamarind ball
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 10-12 small onions (shallots), peeled
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Jaggery or sugar (optional)
  • Vegetables of your choice (optional, like drumsticks, brinjal, etc.)

Procedure

  • Soak tamarind in warm water and extract the juice.
  • Heat sesame oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, shallots, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add slit green chilies and tamarind extract. Mix well.
  • Add sambar powder, turmeric powder, and asafoetida. Mix well.
  • Add salt to taste and jaggery or sugar (optional). Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Add vegetables of your choice if using. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  • Adjust consistency by adding water if needed. Simmer until the kuzhambu thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
