Tomato Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 3 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, minced garlic, and green chili. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until tomatoes are soft and mushy.
  • Add salt to taste and mix well.
  • Add cooked rice to the pan. Mix gently until the rice is well coated with the tomato mixture.
  • Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat to allow flavors to blend.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Tomato Rice!
