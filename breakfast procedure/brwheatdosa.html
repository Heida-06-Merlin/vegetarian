Wheat Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup semolina (optional)
  • 1/4 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • A few curry leaves, finely chopped
  • A handful of coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, rice flour, semolina (if using), chopped green chili, grated ginger, chopped curry leaves, chopped coriander leaves, cumin seeds, and salt.
  • Add curd and mix well to form a smooth batter. Add water gradually to achieve a dosa batter consistency.
  • Cover the batter and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  • Heat a dosa tawa or griddle over medium heat.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot tawa and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  • Drizzle some oil around the edges of the dosa.
  • Cook the dosa until it turns golden brown and crisp on the bottom.
  • Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it is cooked through.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • Serve hot with chutney, sambar, or any side dish of your choice.
