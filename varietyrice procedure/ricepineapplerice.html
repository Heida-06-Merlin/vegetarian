Pineapple Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish, chopped

Procedure

  • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  • Add beaten eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Saut√© minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant and translucent.
  • Add diced bell pepper and cook until slightly softened.
  • Add cooked rice, soy sauce, and oyster sauce (if using). Mix well to combine.
  • Add pineapple chunks and scrambled eggs back to the pan. Stir until everything is heated through.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Drizzle sesame oil over the rice and give it a final stir.
  • Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your Pineapple Rice!
