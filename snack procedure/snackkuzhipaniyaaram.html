Kuzhi Paniyaram

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped green chilies
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for greasing the paniyaram pan

Procedure

  1. Soak raw rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds together for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients along with cooked rice into a smooth batter. The consistency should be like dosa batter.
  3. Add salt to taste and mix well. Allow the batter to ferment overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. After fermentation, heat a paniyaram pan and grease the craters with oil.
  5. Drop a spoonful of batter into each crater, filling them up to 3/4th full.
  6. Sprinkle chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, and grated coconut (if using) on top of each paniyaram.
  7. Cover and cook on medium-low heat for 2-3 minutes or until the bottom turns golden brown.
  8. Flip the paniyarams using a skewer or spoon and cook the other side until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove from the pan and serve hot with chutney or sambar.
