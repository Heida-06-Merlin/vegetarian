Vatha Kuzhambu

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup tamarind pulp
  • 10-12 sundried vegetables (vathal), such as manathakkali vathal or sundakkai vathal
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Soak tamarind pulp in warm water and extract the juice.
  • Heat sesame oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions, crushed garlic, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add sambar powder, turmeric powder, and asafoetida. Mix well.
  • Add tamarind extract, salt, and vathal. Allow it to boil until the raw smell of tamarind disappears and the vathal becomes soft.
  • Simmer for a few more minutes until the kuzhambu thickens to the desired consistency.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
