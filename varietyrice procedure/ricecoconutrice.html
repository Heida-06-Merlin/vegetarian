Coconut Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated coconut
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  • In a large pan, heat coconut oil over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Once they start to splutter, add chopped onion, garlic, ginger, and green chili. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  • Add grated coconut and continue to sauté for another 2 minutes.
  • Stir in turmeric powder and salt.
  • Add rinsed rice to the pan and mix well with the coconut mixture.
  • Pour in coconut milk and water. Stir gently to combine.
  • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and all the liquid is absorbed.
  • Once done, fluff the rice with a fork. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot as a side dish or with your favorite curry. Enjoy your delicious Coconut Rice!
