Milagu Kuzhambu (Pepper Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon-sized tamarind ball
  • 2 tablespoons black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon fenugreek seeds
  • 10-12 dry red chilies
  • 10-12 shallots, peeled
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Soak tamarind in warm water and extract the juice.
  • Dry roast black peppercorns, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, and fenugreek seeds until fragrant. Grind into a fine paste along with dry red chilies.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add peeled shallots and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add tamarind extract, turmeric powder, asafoetida, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  • Add ground spice paste to the pan. Mix well and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Adjust salt if needed. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Milagu Kuzhambu is ready to be served. Serve hot with rice.
