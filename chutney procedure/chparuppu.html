Paruppu Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup split pigeon peas (toor dal)
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add split pigeon peas (toor dal) and dried red chilies. Roast until the dal turns golden brown.
  • Add chopped onion, garlic cloves, grated coconut, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Saut√© until the onions turn translucent.
  • Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down.
  • Transfer the cooled mixture to a blender or grinder. Add salt to taste and grind to a smooth paste, adding water as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Paruppu chutney is ready to be served with dosa, idli, or any South Indian breakfast dish!
