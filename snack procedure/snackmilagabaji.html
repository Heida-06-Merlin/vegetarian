Milagaa Baji

Ingredients

  • 6-8 green bell peppers (milagaa), slit lengthwise and deseeded
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a bowl, mix gram flour, rice flour, cornflour, chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add water gradually to make a thick batter. The batter should coat the back of a spoon well.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
  • Dip each slit green bell pepper into the batter, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  • Carefully drop the coated bell peppers into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the fried milagaa baji using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  • Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
