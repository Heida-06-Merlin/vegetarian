Mullangi Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 4-5 medium-sized radishes (mullangi), washed, peeled, and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped green chilies and sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped radishes, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the radishes are tender.
  • Once the radishes are cooked, add cumin powder and grated coconut. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish with rice or roti.
