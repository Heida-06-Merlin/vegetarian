Karamani Kuzhambu (Black-eyed Peas Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup karamani (black-eyed peas), soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Pressure cook the soaked karamani with enough water until soft. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
  • Now add turmeric powder, sambar powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Dissolve tamarind paste in water and add it to the pan. Let it boil for 5 minutes.
  • Add the cooked karamani and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
