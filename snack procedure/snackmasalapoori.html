Masala Poori

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, semolina, chopped onions, chopped coriander leaves, green chilies, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, yogurt, and salt.
  • Knead the mixture into a stiff dough using water as needed.
  • Cover the dough and let it rest for about 15-20 minutes.
  • Divide the dough into small lemon-sized balls and roll them out into discs (pooris) of about 4-5 inches in diameter.
  • Heat oil for deep frying in a pan over medium heat.
  • Gently slide the rolled out pooris into the hot oil and fry until they puff up and turn golden brown on both sides.
  • Remove the fried pooris using a slotted spoon and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Serve hot with your favorite accompaniment like chutney, pickle, or yogurt.
