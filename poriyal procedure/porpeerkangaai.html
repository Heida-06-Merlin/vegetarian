Peerkangaai Poriyal

Ingredients

  • 500g peerkangaai (ridge gourd), peeled and chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and sauté until it turns golden brown.
  • Add chopped onions and slit green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped ridge gourd (peerkangaai) and curry leaves. Mix well.
  • Add salt to taste. Cover and cook until ridge gourd is tender, stirring occasionally.
  • If using, add grated coconut and mix well. Cook for another minute.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish with rice or roti.
