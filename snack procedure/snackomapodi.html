Omapodi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 2 tablespoons rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine besan, rice flour, ajwain, turmeric powder, asafoetida, and salt.
  • Add water gradually and knead into a smooth dough. The dough should be stiff yet pliable.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Take a portion of the dough and place it into the omapodi maker or a piping bag fitted with a star nozzle.
  • Press the dough directly into the hot oil in a circular motion to form spirals.
  • Fry until the omapodi turns golden brown and crispy. Stir occasionally for even frying.
  • Once done, remove the omapodi from the oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining dough.
  • Allow the omapodi to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
  • Serve omapodi as a crunchy snack with tea or store for later consumption.
