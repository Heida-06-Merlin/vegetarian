Idiyappam

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 1/2 cups hot water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine rice flour and salt.
  • Gradually add hot water while stirring continuously with a spoon.
  • Knead the mixture into a soft, smooth, and non-sticky dough. Add more water if needed.
  • Add oil to the dough and knead again until well combined.
  • Grease the idiyappam maker (string hopper press) with oil.
  • Take a portion of the dough and place it inside the idiyappam maker.
  • Press the dough onto idli plates or greased banana leaves in a circular motion to form idiyappam strings.
  • Steam the idiyappam for about 7-10 minutes until cooked through.
  • Once cooked, remove the idiyappam from the steamer and serve hot with coconut milk, sweetened coconut milk, or any curry of your choice.
