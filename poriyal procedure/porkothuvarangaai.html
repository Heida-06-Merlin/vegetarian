Kothuvarangaai Kootu

Ingredients

  • 250g kothuvarangaai (cluster beans), chopped
  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas), cooked
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until the mustard seeds crackle.
  • Add chopped kothuvarangaai and sauté for a few minutes.
  • Add turmeric powder, asafoetida, and salt. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook until the kothuvarangaai is tender.
  • Add cooked toor dal and mix well.
  • Add grated coconut and stir until well combined.
  • Cook for a few more minutes until everything is heated through and flavors are well blended.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot with rice or chapati.
