Pudalangai Kootu

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized snake gourd (pudalangai), peeled, deseeded, and chopped
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or any cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A sprig of curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated coconut
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Procedure

  • Wash moong dal thoroughly and pressure cook with enough water until soft.
  • In a pan, heat coconut oil. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add cumin seeds, dry red chilies, asafoetida, curry leaves, and green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add chopped snake gourd and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked moong dal and mix well with the snake gourd.
  • Add freshly grated coconut and mix gently.
  • Cook for another 5-7 minutes until the flavors meld together and snake gourd is cooked through.
  • Adjust salt if needed.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice or as a side dish for roti.
