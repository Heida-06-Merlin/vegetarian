Rasam

Ingredients

  • 1 small lemon-sized ball of tamarind
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cooked and mashed toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon rasam powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water

Procedure

  • Soak tamarind in warm water for 15 minutes. Squeeze the tamarind to extract the juice and discard the pulp.
  • In a pot, add tamarind extract, chopped tomatoes, slit green chilies, turmeric powder, salt, and water. Bring to a boil.
  • In a separate pan, heat oil. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add cooked toor dal (pigeon peas) and rasam powder to the pot. Stir well.
  • Once the tomatoes are cooked and the rasam comes to a rolling boil, remove from heat.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot as an appetizer or with rice.
