Uttapam

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dosa batter
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomato
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1-2 finely chopped green chilies (optional)
  • A few chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Procedure

  • Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  • Take a ladleful of dosa batter and spread it evenly on the pan to form a thick pancake.
  • Sprinkle chopped onion, tomato, bell pepper, grated carrot, green chilies (if using), and coriander leaves evenly on the pancake.
  • Press the toppings gently into the batter using a spatula.
  • Drizzle some oil or ghee around the edges of the uttapam and cook until the bottom turns golden brown and crisp.
  • Flip the uttapam and cook the other side until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Repeat with the remaining batter to make more uttapam.
  • Serve hot with coconut chutney, tomato chutney, or sambar.
