Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Procedure

  • Rinse rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds together. Soak them in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  • Drain the water and transfer the soaked ingredients to a blender. Grind into a smooth batter, adding water as needed. The batter should have a pouring consistency.
  • Transfer the batter to a large bowl. Add salt and mix well. Allow the batter to ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or until it doubles in volume.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Once hot, pour a ladleful of batter onto the center of the skillet and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin layer.
  • Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa. Cook until the bottom side turns golden brown and crispy.
  • Flip the dosa using a spatula and cook for another minute or until the other side is cooked through.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter to make more dosas.
  • Serve hot dosas with coconut chutney, sambar, or any desired accompaniment.
