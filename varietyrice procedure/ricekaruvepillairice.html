Karuvepillai Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 1 cup fresh curry leaves
  • 3-4 green chilies
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal and chana dal. Saute until they turn golden brown.
  • Add cumin seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saute for a minute.
  • Add turmeric powder and asafoetida. Mix well.
  • Add grated coconut and saute for a couple of minutes.
  • Add cooked rice and salt. Mix well until everything is combined.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your flavorful Karuvepillai Rice!
