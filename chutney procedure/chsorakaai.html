Sorakkai Thogayal

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized sorakkai (bottle gourd), peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind pulp
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add urad dal, dry red chilies, mustard seeds, and cumin seeds. Sauté until the dal turns golden brown.
  • Add chopped sorakkai and sauté until it becomes soft and cooked.
  • Allow it to cool down to room temperature.
  • In a blender, add the cooked sorakkai, grated coconut, tamarind pulp, salt, and a pinch of asafoetida.
  • Grind everything into a smooth paste. Add water if necessary to adjust the consistency.
  • Transfer the thogayal to a serving bowl.
  • For tempering, heat a tablespoon of oil in a small pan.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
  • Add curry leaves and sauté for a few seconds.
  • Pour this tempering over the thogayal and mix well.
  • Your delicious Sorakkai Thogayal is ready to be served with rice or as a side dish!
