Poosanikaai Kootu

Ingredients

  • 500g poosanikaai (yellow pumpkin), diced
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (split yellow lentils), washed
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Pressure cook moong dal with diced poosanikaai until soft and mushy. Set aside.
  • In a separate pan, heat coconut oil.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add cumin seeds, dry red chilies, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a minute.
  • Add cooked moong dal and poosanikaai to the pan. Mix well.
  • In a blender, grind grated coconut, green chilies, and turmeric powder into a fine paste.
  • Add the coconut paste to the pan. Mix well and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  • Adjust salt according to taste.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice or chapati.
