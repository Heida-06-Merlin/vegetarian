Mullu Thenguzhal

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon black sesame seeds
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon melted ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, cumin seeds, black sesame seeds, chopped green chilies, and salt.
  • Heat the coconut milk until warm. Add it gradually to the rice flour mixture and knead into a smooth dough. Add melted ghee and knead again.
  • Fill the dough into a kitchen press fitted with a star-shaped plate (mullu thenguzhal achu).
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Press out the dough in circular patterns directly into the hot oil. Fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove from oil using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining dough.
  • Once cooled, store Mullu Thenguzhal in an airtight container.
  • Serve as a snack with tea or coffee.
