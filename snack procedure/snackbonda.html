Bonda

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine mashed potatoes, chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, cilantro, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Gradually add chickpea flour (besan) to the mixture and knead to form a smooth dough. If needed, sprinkle a little water to bind the mixture together.
  • Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls. Set aside.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Once the oil is hot, carefully drop the prepared potato balls into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  • Remove the bondas from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
