Ellu Thogayal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sesame seeds (white or black)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut (fresh or desiccated)
  • 1 small lemon-sized tamarind ball
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil (preferably sesame oil)

Procedure

  • Dry roast sesame seeds in a pan over medium heat until they turn golden brown and aromatic. Set aside to cool.
  • In the same pan, add a teaspoon of oil and roast dried red chilies, urad dal, and chana dal until they turn golden brown. Set aside to cool.
  • Grind the roasted sesame seeds, roasted dal mixture, grated coconut, tamarind, garlic cloves, and salt into a smooth paste using a blender or mixer grinder. Use a little water if needed.
  • Heat the remaining oil in a small pan and temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves (optional).
  • Add the ground paste to the pan and saut√© for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears and the thogayal thickens slightly.
  • Remove from heat and let it cool before serving.
  • Ellu Thogayal is ready to be served with rice or as a side dish for idli or dosa.
