Kathirikai Bajji

Ingredients

  • 2 large brinjals (eggplants)
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tablespoon rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • Wash and slice the brinjals into thin rounds.
  • In a bowl, mix gram flour, rice flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add water gradually to make a smooth batter with a thick consistency.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Dip the brinjal slices into the batter, ensuring they are well coated.
  • Carefully drop the coated brinjal slices into the hot oil.
  • Fry until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  • Remove from oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
