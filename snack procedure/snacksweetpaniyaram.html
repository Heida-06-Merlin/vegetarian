Sweet Paniyaram

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup jaggery, grated
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • Oil for frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, add rice flour, grated jaggery, grated coconut, cardamom powder, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  • Add yogurt gradually to the mixture and mix until you get a thick batter consistency. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  • Heat the paniyaram pan and grease the molds with ghee.
  • Pour spoonfuls of batter into each mold, filling them up to 3/4th full.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes until the bottom side turns golden brown.
  • Flip each paniyaram using a skewer or spoon and cook until the other side is golden brown and the paniyarams are cooked through.
  • Remove from the pan and serve hot.
