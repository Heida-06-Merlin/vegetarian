Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dosa rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • 1/4 cup chana dal
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Procedure

  • Wash and soak dosa rice, urad dal, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds together in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  • Drain the water and grind the soaked mixture into a smooth batter using a wet grinder or a high-speed blender, adding water as needed to achieve a thick pouring consistency. The batter should be smooth and without any grittiness.
  • Transfer the batter to a large bowl, add salt, and mix well. Allow the batter to ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or until it doubles in volume and becomes slightly sour.
  • Heat a non-stick dosa tawa or griddle over medium heat.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter onto the center of the tawa and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges.
  • Cook the dosa until the edges start to lift and turn golden brown. Flip the dosa and cook the other side for a few seconds until it turns golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the dosa from the tawa and serve hot with potato masala filling and coconut chutney.
