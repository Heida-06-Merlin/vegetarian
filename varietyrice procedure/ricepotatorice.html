Potato Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably cooled
  • 2 large potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.
  • Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onion, garlic, and green chili. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  • Add diced potatoes and cook until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice to the pan and gently mix with the potato mixture until well combined.
  • Cover and cook for a few minutes until the flavors are well combined.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Potato Rice!
