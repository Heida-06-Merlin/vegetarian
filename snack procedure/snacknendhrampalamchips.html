Nendhram Pazham Chips

Ingredients

  • 4-5 ripe nendhram pazham (banana)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: pinch of turmeric powder

Procedure

  • Peel the bananas and slice them thinly using a mandoline or a sharp knife. Aim for uniformly thin slices.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat.
  • Once the oil is hot, carefully add the banana slices to the hot oil in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  • Fry the banana slices until they turn golden brown and crisp, stirring occasionally for even cooking. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.
  • Using a slotted spoon, remove the fried banana chips from the oil and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • While the chips are still warm, sprinkle them with salt and optionally, turmeric powder, tossing gently to coat evenly.
  • Allow the chips to cool completely before storing them in an airtight container.
  • Your homemade Nendhram Pazham Chips are ready to enjoy as a crispy and delicious snack!
