Capsicum Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized capsicums (green or red), chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add urad dal, chana dal, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, and asafoetida. Sauté until dals turn golden brown.
  • Add chopped garlic and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped capsicums and salt. Mix well and cook until capsicums are soft.
  • Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down.
  • Transfer the cooled mixture to a blender. Add tamarind paste.
  • Blend into a smooth chutney consistency. Adjust salt if needed.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Serve with dosa, idli, or any South Indian breakfast dish.
