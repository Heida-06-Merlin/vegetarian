Nei Appam

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup jaggery, grated
  • 1/2 cup coconut, grated
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • Water, as needed

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, grated jaggery, grated coconut, mashed banana, cardamom powder, baking soda, and a pinch of salt.
  • Add water gradually and mix well to form a thick batter. The batter should have a dropping consistency.
  • Heat a paniyaram pan (appam pan) and add a few drops of ghee into each mold.
  • Pour the batter into each mold until it is about 3/4 full.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for a few minutes until the bottom side turns golden brown.
  • Flip each appam using a skewer or spoon and cook the other side until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Remove the nei appam from the pan and serve hot.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more ghee to the pan as needed.
