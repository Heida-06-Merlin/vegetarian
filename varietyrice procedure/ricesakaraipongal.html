Sarkarai Pongal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (split green gram)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), chopped
  • 1/2 cup jaggery (sarkarai), grated
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 cup cashews and raisins, mixed
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron (optional)

Procedure

  • Wash rice and moong dal together and set aside.
  • In a pressure cooker, add washed rice, moong dal, mixed vegetables, and water. Pressure cook until well cooked.
  • Once cooked, mash the mixture lightly.
  • In a separate pan, melt jaggery with a little water to make a syrup.
  • Add the jaggery syrup to the cooked rice-dal mixture and mix well.
  • Heat ghee in a small pan, fry cashews and raisins until golden brown, and add to the pongal.
  • Add cardamom powder and saffron (if using) to the pongal and mix well.
  • Simmer for a few minutes until everything is well combined and aromatic.
  • Sarkarai Pongal is ready to be served hot!
