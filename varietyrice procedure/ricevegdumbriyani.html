Veg Dum Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, cauliflower), diced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices:
    • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp turmeric
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • Salt to taste
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fried onions (optional)
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp biryani masala powder
  • Whole spices:
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 4 cloves
    • 4 green cardamom pods
    • 1-inch cinnamon stick

Procedure

  • Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  • Add mixed vegetables, chopped tomatoes, and cook until vegetables are slightly tender.
  • In a bowl, mix yogurt with red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Add this mixture to the pan and cook until the oil separates.
  • In a separate pot, boil water and add soaked basmati rice along with salt and a bay leaf. Cook until the rice is 70% done. Drain the water and keep the rice aside.
  • In a heavy-bottomed pan or biryani pot, layer the partially cooked rice and the vegetable mixture alternatively. Sprinkle chopped mint leaves, coriander leaves, and fried onions between the layers. Cover with a tight-fitting lid.
  • Dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  • Gently mix the layers before serving. Serve hot with raita or salad.
