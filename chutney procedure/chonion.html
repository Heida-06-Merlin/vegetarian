Onion Chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed

Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and urad dal. Let them splutter and turn golden brown.
  • Add dried red chilies and sliced onions. Sauté until onions turn translucent and slightly browned.
  • Add garlic cloves and continue sautéing for another minute.
  • Add tamarind paste and salt. Mix well.
  • Remove from heat and allow the mixture to cool.
  • Transfer the cooled mixture to a blender. Add a little water and blend into a smooth paste.
  • Adjust seasoning if needed.
  • Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
  • Serve with dosa, idli, or any other snack of your choice.
