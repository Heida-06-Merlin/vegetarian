Vella Seedai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered jaggery (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon melted ghee
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  • In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour, powdered jaggery, grated coconut, melted ghee, and cardamom powder.
  • Knead the mixture into a smooth dough. If needed, add a little water or more ghee to bind the dough.
  • Pinch small portions of the dough and roll them into small balls or seedai shapes.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Gently drop the seedai into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crisp.
  • Remove the fried seedai using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Allow the seedai to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
  • Vella Seedai is ready to be served as a festive snack!
