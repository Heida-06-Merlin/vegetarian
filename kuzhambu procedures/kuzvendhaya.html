Vendhaya Kuzhambu (Fenugreek Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup fenugreek seeds (vendhayam)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (for tempering)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon sambar powder
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure

  • Soak fenugreek seeds in water for 4-6 hours or overnight.
  • Grind the soaked fenugreek seeds to a smooth paste using a little water.
  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and fenugreek seeds for tempering. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
  • Now add turmeric powder, sambar powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Dissolve tamarind paste in water and add it to the pan. Let it boil for 5 minutes.
  • Add the ground fenugreek paste and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
